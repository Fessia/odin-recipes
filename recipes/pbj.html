Classic Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingridients

  • 2 slices of White Bread
  • 4oz of Peanut Butter
  • 2oz of Jelly

Directions

  1. Using a butter knife, spread Peanut Butter across one slice of bread
  2. Using a butter knife, spread Jelly across the other slice of bread
  3. Put both slices of bread together with their contents facing each other
  4. Enjoy!
